About

Math is my passion, and my craft.

I hold an engineering degree from NIT Warangal, one of India's most rigorous institutions. After years of competitive math, I chose to spend my days doing what I love most: guiding high schoolers through the subject that once changed my life.

I've taught students from the bustling classrooms of Texas, the sunny coasts of Florida, the rolling hills of West Virginia, the relentless drive of New Jersey, the vibrant cities of Canada and London, and the heart of India.

Every lesson is tailored to the student's strengths, their gaps, and the exam they're aiming at. The result isn't just better scores; it's a student who finally likes math.
