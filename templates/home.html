About

Math is my passion, and my craft.

Math is my passion

I'm Karthikeya Kusuma. I proudly hold an engineering degree from NIT Warangal, one of India's most prestigious institutions. I've embarked on a mission to guide and inspire high school students to turn their dreams into reality. My goal? To help them secure a spot at their dream university.

Students taught

I have guided over 20 high school students worldwide—from the bustling classrooms of Texas, the sunny coasts of North and South Florida, and the rolling hills of West Virginia, along with the infamous New Jersey to the vibrant cities of Canada, and London and the dynamic communities of India.

Through personalized one-on-one mentorship, I have helped students not only master complex mathematical concepts but also build confidence, develop problem-solving skills, and cultivate a lifelong passion for learning. My approach focuses on tailoring lessons to each student's strengths and challenges, ensuring they achieve academic excellence while gaining a deeper appreciation for the subject.
